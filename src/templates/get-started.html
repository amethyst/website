Thank you for stopping by.

The book will guide you through your first steps in Amethyst. It will teach you the basics: how the architecture of Amethyst works, how to create a new project, and demonstrate some of the most important features of the engine.

Then, or alternatively, you can check out the Amethyst examples to get a deeper understanding of what an Amethyst project looks like. This will also get you accustomed to the Entity Component System architecture if this concept is new to you.

In the mean time, if you encounter any issue, have feedback to give or simply want to say hi, you can reach out for the community! You can reach us on Reddit, Discord or our community forums, and we'll be glad to help you out! (we're super nice)
